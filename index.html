About GATE

The Global Association Theological Education (GATE) is a premier academic entry-point for theological study. Designed as both a qualifying credential and a preparatory examination for seminary and divinity school admission, GATE assesses candidates across the full spectrum of systematic theology, biblical studies, and Christian philosophy.

All examinations are passage-based — each question is anchored to a KJV Scripture passage, requiring careful reading, comprehension, and theological analysis. This methodology mirrors the academic rigor of postgraduate theological assessment.

GATE credentials are recognized as a qualifying threshold for Bachelor-level theological studies and ministry certification programs. The platform operates entirely client-side — no server required, no data transmitted externally.
