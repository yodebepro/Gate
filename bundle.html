Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade from Basic to Premium later?

Yes. Purchase the Premium bundle and confirm your existing email — your tier will upgrade automatically and all your previous scores are retained.

How many times can I retake the exams?

Unlimited retakes are included with your bundle. Each attempt is logged in your history.

Do I need an internet connection during the exam?

No. Once the platform files are downloaded, all 1,500 questions are embedded locally. No internet is required to take examinations.

What is the passing score?

65% or above is considered a Pass. 80%+ is Merit, 90%+ is Distinction.

Can the administrator grant access without payment?

Yes. Administrators can grant Basic or Premium access to any registered user directly from the Admin Console.
